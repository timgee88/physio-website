Services

Here at Arcola Physiotherapy & Acupuncture, we offer a wide range of services to help you get back to doing the things you love to do.

Physiotherapy

Our physical therapist will provide a detailed assessment which includes asking questions surrounding your injury and performing various physical tests and maneuvers. A personalized treatment plan will then be provided, consisting of various modalities and exercises to help strengthen and restore your mobility and function.

Acupuncture

Acupuncture involves inserting very thing needles through the skin at specific points in the body to provide therapeutic effect. Developed thousands of years ago in ancient China, it is now widely practiced in the West. Acupuncture has been used to treat a variety of ailments includin headaches, back and neck pain and various types of chronic pain.

Massage Therapy

Massage therapy involves hands on manipulation of soft tissues in the body to provide therapeutic effect. Our massage therapist can help provide pain relief and promote relaxation.
